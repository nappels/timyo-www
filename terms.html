Privacy Policy

Posted: November 1, 2015

Thanks for downloading Timyo! Here we describe how we collect, use and handle your information when you use our Service. The Service includes a software application users download to a device and a service that processes data on our servers.

Which information we collect and for what kind of usage

Email Credentials: We collect and process your email address and credentials to provide you the Service.Timyo does not use your email credentials for any other purpose. To learn more about how we process your email credentials please see Timyo Security Policy. We collect and process your email address to provide you the Service, carry out transactions you authorize and communicate with you.

Email Data: Timyo has no access to your email contents and does nos store any information related of the content of your emails. We only process your email messages to provide you the Service.

With whom

We are not in the business of selling or renting users data. We will not share data with third parties outside Timyo and affiliates except in the following narrow circumstances: Others working for Timyo.

Timyo uses certain trusted third parties to help us provide, improve, protect, and promote our Services. These third parties will access your information only to perform tasks on our behalf and in compliance with this Privacy Policy.

Support: If you choose to utilize support services, you may be asked to provide certain information (type of email account, type of device, description of usage etc). We will never ask for, nor should you ever send us the password to your email account. All communication with our support team is completely private, and we never use information collected for uses not related to support and application improvement.

Usage Statistics: We collect anonymous usage statistics to help us improve Timyo. For example, we track which screens are viewed most frequently and which features are utilized most frequently. These statistics contain no personally identifiable information.

If we sell or reorganize all or part of our business, we may need to disclose user information to a third party who acquires our assets, as permitted by law.

Law & Order: We may disclose your information to third parties if we determine that such disclosure is reasonably necessary to (a) comply with the law; (b) protect any person from death or serious bodily injury; (c) prevent fraud or abuse of Timyo or our users; or (d) protect Timyo's property rights.

Data Location

The information we collect and process may be stored and processed in the United States or any other country where we or our parent company, affiliates or service providers maintain facilities. Information are currently stored and processed in the E.U., in high available (Tier IV) datacenters, managed by ISO 27001 organization. Location might change in respect with the above policy without further notice.

Changes

We may revise this Privacy Policy from time to time, and will post the most current version on our website. If a revision meaningfully reduces your rights, we will notify you.

Contact

Have questions or concerns about Timyo, our Services and privacy? Contact privacy@timyo.com
